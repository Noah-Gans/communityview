Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Community View ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Community View mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Community View is designed to provide GIS mapping and property search services. We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)

Usage Data

We may collect information about how you access and use the App, including:

  • Search queries and property searches
  • Features accessed and used
  • App performance and error logs

Location Data

The App may access your device's location to provide mapping services. Location data is used locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use location-based features. You can disable location services in your device settings at any time.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our App
  • To process your account registration and manage your subscription
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve and optimize the App
  • To send you important updates about the App (if you opt in)

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase - For authentication and data storage
  • Stripe - For payment processing (payment information is handled by Stripe and not stored by us)
  • Mapbox - For mapping services

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our App (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our App is appropriate for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
